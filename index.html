About Me

Multidisciplinary Engineer with expertise in web development (React, Node.js, Python/Django), app development (React Native, Flutter), and laboratory research (biochemistry, materials science). I'm driven by a deep curiosity to understand how things work and a passion for building solutions that improve lives.

My journey has been a blend of diving deep into code and exploring the intricacies of scientific research. I thrive on the challenge of bridging the gap between abstract concepts and tangible results. Whether I'm debugging a complex algorithm or analyzing experimental data, I approach every task with a blend of analytical rigor and creative problem-solving.

Beyond the lab and the keyboard, I'm an avid learner and a firm believer in the power of continuous growth. I enjoy exploring new technologies, but I also find inspiration in hiking, reading, and engaging in thoughtful conversations. I believe that a well-rounded life fuels creativity and innovation. I'm particularly passionate about projects that have a positive social or environmental impact, and I'm always eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals to make a difference. I believe that engineering is not just about building things, but about building a better future.
